The Intelligent Investor

By Benjamin Graham

Description

The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham is widely considered the definitive work on value investing. First published in 1949, it provides a long-term strategy on how to avoid substantial errors and achieve long-term investment goals. Graham emphasizes the importance of investing with a margin of safety and viewing stocks as ownership in a business.

Book Details

  • Genre: Investing, Finance, Business
  • Publication Date: 1949
  • Number of Pages: Approximately 600
  • Author Name: Benjamin Graham
