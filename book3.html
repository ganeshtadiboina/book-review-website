Millionaire Fast Lane

By MJ DeMarco

Description

The Millionaire Fastlane is a book by MJ DeMarco that challenges the traditional "slowlane" approach to wealth building (get a job, save, invest in the stock market over 40 years). DeMarco argues for an entrepreneurial path focused on creating businesses that have the potential for rapid wealth accumulation. The book outlines principles and strategies for building such "fastlane" businesses.

Book Details

  • Genre: Business, Entrepreneurship, Personal Finance
  • Publication Date: 2011
  • Number of Pages: Approximately 320
  • Author Name: MJ DeMarco
